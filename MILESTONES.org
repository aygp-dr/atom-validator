** Schematron Rules

Express constraints RelaxNG cannot:

#+begin_src xml
<sch:rule context="atom:entry">
  <sch:assert test="atom:content or atom:link[@rel='alternate']">
    Entry must have content or alternate link
  </sch:assert>
</sch:rule>
#+end_src
